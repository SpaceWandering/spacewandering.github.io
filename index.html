Space Wandering

Space Wandering is a collection of unique and collectible NFTs on the Polygon Blockchain.

The Project

The collection is composed of thousands of different pixel art planets. Some planets are randomly generated using a set of traits, each one with its own rarity, while others are hand-drawn and have unique traits. Get ready to venture out in the depth of the Universe, and explore the rich lore build around the NFTs.
